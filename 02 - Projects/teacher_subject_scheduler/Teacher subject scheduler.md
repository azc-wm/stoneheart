## Purpose

> A tool that allows teacher to distribute rule-based teaching criteria and subject units

## Goals
- [ ] Enable users to "one-click setup" a subject curriculum based on schedule slots
- [ ] Allow for "feedback cycle/session logging" for official docs on end of academic year
- [ ] RBAC must be in place, owner of resource, collaborators and viewers.

## Non-goals
- [ ] Attendance or alumni data (GDPR Alarm) on v1
- [ ] School schedule generation (we are not a scheduling engine)
- [ ] Out of spain gtm (on v1).
